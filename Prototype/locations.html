Merriton Landing
Bordering Yellow Leaf Bay is the vibrant town of Merriton Landing which is home to many of the activities found in Taniti. This rapidly expanding town is home to may pubs, microbreweries, and dance clubs. There are also helicopter rides for aerial tours of the area.
Tanitian Rainforest

The Tanitian rainforest is a unique ecosystem that is home to several species of plants and animals. It also contains serveral rivers and springs which are key fresh water sources for the Tanitian people.

Tanitian Volcano
A popular tourist spot is the great Tanitian Volcano. This active volcano steals the Taniti skyline and attracts visitors for all over the world.

Sunshine Grocery
Sunshine Grocery is Taniti's largest grocery and pharmacy store. With a huge variety of foods and cosmetics, Sunshine is sure to have what you need.
Kala 24 Hour Convenience Store

Palm Place Market
The Palm Place market is often a popular tourist destination due to it's unique island fruits and vegetables at affordable prices. All produce is organically grown locally.
Tropical Oasis Hotel

Tropical Oasis Hotel is a true island getaway residing on the Tanitian coast and close to Taniti City. The Hotel room includes free breakfast and the lounge is open all night.

Taniti Special Microbrewery
This local microbrewery is independently owned and family run. Taniti Special is a favorite spot for visitors looking for good quality craft beer.
